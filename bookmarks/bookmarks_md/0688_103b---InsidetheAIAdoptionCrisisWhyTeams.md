# [AI Is Tearing Companies Apart](https://go.writer.com/hubfs/pdfs/generative-ai-adoption-enterprise-survey-writer-com.pdf)

A survey of 1,600 U.S. knowledge workers just exposed what’s really happening inside AI transformation projects—and it’s not pretty.

-   42% of execs say AI is creating division, not progress.

-   71% admit AI is happening in silos—no alignment, no impact.

-   41% of Millennials and Gen Z are actively resisting AI rollouts.

-   35% of employees are buying their own AI tools—they don’t trust company systems.

-   27% are using unapproved “shadow AI,” opening major security risks.

-   94% of execs feel let down by their AI vendors.

**Why important?** Most companies aren’t failing at AI—they’re failing at **change management**. Pushing tools without trust, vision, or collaboration doesn’t lead to transformation. It leads to chaos. If employees don’t believe in the strategy, if teams stay in silos, and if leaders chase hype over execution
